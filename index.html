LumeLink's three-port design offers a safer and more durable approach to UART debugging for USB-C devices.

Port Configuration

    One port passes through completely, while the second connects to the UART chip

How ChargeLink Works

ChargeLink uses a unique approach to establish a data connection over standard charging pins, eliminating the need for special programming interfaces.

Technical Operation

  1. Connect the USB-C port to your computer
  2. Connect the USB-A port to your device's charging port
  3. The UART chip processes data from your computer
  4. Signal sent through the charging pins to the device
  5. Device interprets this signal as firmware data
  6. Firmware is transferred through standard charging connection
